## Clauses for ALL Grain:

1. **Previous Cargo Restriction:**
   "LAST 3 CARGOES: CLEAN/HARMLESS/ODORLESS ONLY. NO FERTILIZERS, CHEMICALS, COAL, OR OTHER CONTAMINATING CARGOES"

2. **Trimming:**
   "CARGO TO BE PROPERLY TRIMMED, LEVELED AND STOWED IN ACCORDANCE WITH IMSBC CODE"

3. **Surveyor Access:**
   "CHRTS/RCVRS/SHPRS SURVEYORS TO HAVE FREE AND UNRESTRICTED ACCESS TO VSSL AND HOLDS AT LOAD AND DISCHARGE PORTS FOR INSPECTION, SAMPLING AND SUPERVISION"

4. **Documents (Egypt):**
   "TIME FROM ALL ONBOARD DOCS READY AND UP TO COMPLETION OF DISCHARGING EIU"

## STW Verification Warning

IMPORTANT: Always verify STW with charterer!
Different crop grades and moisture content can affect STW.
Database values are typical ranges only.
